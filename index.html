Our Services

Refining & Assaying

We offer highly efficient and affordable solutions for recycling old jewelry and unsold stock items. Our lab conducts gold assaying, metal analysis, and R&D to ensure metal quality via fire assay and spectrometry.

Smelting

Smelting is an essential process used to produce high-quality metals. We use intense heat to either melt fine metal into bars or convert scrap metal into grains, transforming raw materials into products our customers need.

Bullion Trading

We manufacture an extensive range of gold and silver bullion that caters to diverse market demands. Each bar undergoes rigorous inspections and precise weighing before packaging and dispatch, ensuring complete accuracy.

Minting Coins & Bars

Our die-making and minting division employs state-of-the-art machinery and cutting-edge technology to produce coins, medals, and bespoke items with a comprehensive collection of decorative motifs and designs.

Precious Stones Separation

Our proprietary process for extracting diamonds and precious stones from finished jewelry is highly efficient. We carefully soften the metal and handpick each stone, ensuring no damage during extraction.
